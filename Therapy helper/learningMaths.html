Working Math Skills


Some kids with developmental disabilities require to work in concrete activities in order to gain math skills.


It is necessary to start counting objects, the main focus would be to practice with objects and images. Help them to understand the relation between the words (one, two, etc) with the numbers (1, 2, etc)


With this material, make sure the child counts the objects and help him to label the numbers. Use ecoics at the beginning.
